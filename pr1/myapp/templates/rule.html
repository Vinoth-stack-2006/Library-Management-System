Rules and Regulations of Central Library – for CIET Students

General Rules:

  1. The Library will be kept open from 8.30 a.m. to 4.45 p.m. on all working days.
  2. All students should become members of the college library by filling the appropriate forms.
  3. All students should come to the Library in proper dress code without fail.
  4. All students must sign the Gate Register before entering the Library.
  5. All students are requested to keep their belongings outside.
  6. All users are instructed to take notes from the Reference Books/Text Books/Journals/Magazines/Newspapers/Back Volumes/Project Reports, etc. using loose sheets only.
  7. Strict Silence must be observed in and around the Library. Consultation, discussions, or unnecessary chatting is prohibited. The Librarian may refuse the issue of books to such students.
  8. Usage of mobile phones is strictly prohibited inside the Library.
  9. Books can be searched using the OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue) system.
  10. Books should be handled properly and kept covered.
  11. Students must not mark, underline, or scribble in the books.
  12. Students should check books at the time of issue for damages. If any damage is found, it must be reported immediately. Otherwise, the last borrower will be held responsible and may be required to pay compensation.
  13. Folding, tearing, or underlining pages will be seriously viewed, and penalties will be imposed.
  14. The Librarian reserves the right to recall any book at any time.
  15. Reference Books must be used inside the Library only and will not be lent for home reading.
  16. Books, Journals, and Magazines after use must be replaced in their proper places.

Administration Rules:

  • The maximum number of books that can be taken by each UG student is 4 and by each PG student is 8.
  • Books to be borrowed or renewed must be produced at the counter along with the identity card.

— Librarian
